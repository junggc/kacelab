Welcome to
KOLON BIOTECH

KOLON Biotech is a reliable cell and gene therapy specialized CDMO.
Our experts with experience in the production of existing cell and gene therapy
and exosome therapy products go through the entire process in product manufacturing.
We are committed to operational excellence in GMP manufacturing
and quality assurance to deliver value to our customers.

End-to-End
Service

KOLON Biotech offer integrated end-to-end services starting from tech transfer, process development, production, and quality to logistics. Clients may select one or multiple
